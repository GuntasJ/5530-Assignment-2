This dataset is about diabetes among pregnant women who are at risk of diabetes

The features are the following:

Pregnancies (int) => How many preganancies they have had before

Glucose (int) => Their blood glucose

BloodPressure (int) => Their blood pressure

SkinThickness (int) => How thick their skin is, measured in mm

Insulin (int) => Their blood insulin

BMI (float) => Their BMI

DiabetesPedigreeFunction (float) => Likelyhood of diabetes

Age (int) How old they are

Outcome (int) Prediction of if they have diabetes or not
