This dataset contains deatils and attributes associated of used cars in India.

The features are the following:

Name (String) => the name of the car

Location (String) => where the car is located

Year (int) => what year the make of the car is

Kilometers_Driven (int) => how many kilometers the car has been driven

Fuel_Type (Category expressed in terms of Diesel, Electric, and Petrol) => The type of fuel that car uses

Transmission (Category expressed in terms of Automatic and Manual) => The transmission type

Owner_Type (Category expressed in terms of First, Second, Third, and Fourth and above) => How many times the car has been used by different owners

Mileage (float with two different measurements, on in km/kg and the other in klpl) => How fuel efficient the car is

Engine (int measured in cc) => The speed of the engine

Power (float measured in bhp) => The power of the car

Seats (float) => How many seats the car has

New_Price (float measured in cr and lakhs) => The predicted new price of the car

Price (float, measurement not given) => The original price of the car when it was still new
